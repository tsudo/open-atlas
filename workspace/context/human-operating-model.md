## How to Fill This In

Not sure what to write? Try this exercise:

1. **Think about the last 5 times you corrected your AI.** What was the pattern? That's a preference worth documenting.
2. **Think about your best collaboration.** What made it work? Write those conditions down.
3. **Think about what wastes your time.** What do you keep doing that you wish someone would catch? That's a friction point.

You don't need to fill in every section right away. Start with Core Cognitive Mode and Decision Principles — those have the highest impact. Add the rest as you notice patterns.
