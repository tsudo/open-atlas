# Strategy: Self-Refine

**When to use:** Drafting, writing, content creation, any output that benefits from iteration.

**When NOT to use:** Factual extraction, analysis where the first answer is the right answer.

---

## Instruction

1. Produce an initial draft addressing the core requirements
2. Critique your own draft: what's weak, unclear, or missing?
3. Revise based on your critique — don't just append, rewrite the weak parts
4. If a third pass would materially improve quality, do it. Otherwise stop.

Deliver the final refined version, not the intermediate drafts. If you revised significantly, note what changed and why in a brief footnote.
