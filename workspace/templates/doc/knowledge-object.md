## Lifecycle

Knowledge objects follow a promotion path:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `draft` | Created, not yet reviewed |
| `reviewed` | Checked for correctness and completeness |
| `canonical` | Preferred reference — stable truth |
| `archived` | No longer active, retained for history |

**Rule:** AI may draft knowledge objects. Humans promote them to `reviewed` or `canonical`. No automated promotion.
