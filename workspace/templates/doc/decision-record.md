# Decision Record

A structured template for documenting decisions worth preserving. Use when a meaningful choice was made between alternatives and the reasoning should survive the conversation.

**The test:** If someone reads this six months from now, will they understand why things work this way?

---

- What forced this decision now?
- What is the current state that is inadequate?
- What happens if no decision is made?
